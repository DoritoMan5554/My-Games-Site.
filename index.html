WELCOME

Here are a bunch of the games I made
Go check them out on itch.io

WHAP!

This game is a little rage game I made for fun.
There are cosmetics and colors you can customize.
The only control is point and click.

Jump Master

I made this game for a game jam.
It is a little puzzle platformer where you control two
players at once. You also have a limited number
of jumps so use them wisely.

Wonky Karts

This game is a fun racing game that is solely physics based.
Race around on 3 different tracks with your friends.
Also make sure to unlock the cosmetics.

Day in the Life of a Grim Reaper

In this game you decide where people go based on their
good and bad deeds. This game is also one that I made
for a game jam. Pretty simple controls just point and click.

Wave Rider

Another really simple game I made for a game jam.
Just dodge the objects and collect coins.
Use A and D to move or the left and right arrows.

Time Lock

This game is a puzzle platformer where you
can stop time to move around boxes. Its
very short with only 5 levels but pretty fun.
